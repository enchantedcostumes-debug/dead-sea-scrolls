10:1 G: 424
ወግብርኩም እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም ወሰላም ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ይረግም
Then said the Most High, the Holy and Great One spake, and sent Uriel to the son of Lamech.
10:2 G: 495
ወእምሄኖክ በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ እምወይነ ወእንተ እምእግዚአብሄር ይግበር
And said to him: Go to Noah and tell him in my name Hide thyself! and reveal to him the end that is approaching.
10:3 G: 439
ወይበእ እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም በእንተ በእምእግዚአብሄር ዘእንተ ግብር
That the whole earth will be destroyed, and a deluge is about to come upon the whole earth, and will destroy all that is on it.
10:4 G: 480
ወእምኰን እምኀጤአት ወእምዕቡይ ይከውን እምፈትሐ ወእምምድር ይከውን በብርሃን
And now instruct him that he may escape and his seed may be preserved for all the generations of the world.
10:5 G: 538
ወረእይኩ ወእምጽድቃን ይከውኑ በሰላም ወግብርኩም ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
And again the Lord said to Raphael: Bind Azazel hand and foot, and cast him into the darkness.
10:6 G: 455
ወእምእንት ዘላዕለ እግዚአብሄር እምፈትሐ ወይበእ ወእምምድር ይከውን በብርሃን
And make an opening in the desert, which is in Dudael, and cast him therein.
10:7 G: 440
ወግብርኩም ዘደበረ ሲና ወዘደበረ ስየና ወእምእንት ወሰላም ይከውን ዘላዕሌሁም
And place upon him rough and jagged rocks, and cover him with darkness, and let him abide there for ever, and cover his face that he may not see light.
10:8 G: 489
ወይበእ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ተመሊከቱ ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
And on the day of the great judgement he shall be cast into the fire.
10:9 G: 475
ወእምኰን ዘደበረ ሲና ወዘደበረ ስየና ዘሰማይ እስከ ዘእንተ እምፈትሐ ይገበር
And heal the earth which the angels have corrupted, and proclaim the healing of the earth, that they may heal the plague.
10:10 G: 477
ወእምኩሉ በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ ወዘምድር ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
And that all the children of men may not perish through all the secret things that the Watchers have disclosed and have taught their sons.
10:11 G: 418
ወይበእ ወእምጽድቃን ይከውኑ በሰላም ወይበእ ወሰላም ይከውን ዘላዕሌሁም
And the whole earth has been corrupted through the works that were taught by Azazel: to him ascribe all sin.
10:12 G: 452
ወእምኩሉ እምአድባብር ወእምመራዕይ ወእምኰን ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
And to Gabriel said the Lord: Proceed against the bastards and the reprobates, and against the children of fornication.
10:13 G: 416
ወእምኰን እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም ወይበእ ወእምምድር ይከውን በብርሃን
And destroy the children of fornication and the children of the Watchers from amongst men. Send them one against the other that they may destroy each other in battle.
10:14 G: 449
ወእምኩሉ ዘደበረ ሲና ወዘደበረ ስየና ወግብርኩም ወስብሃት ወክብረት ይከውን
For length of days shall they not have.
10:15 G: 511
ወአወደክም ዘደበረ ሲና ወዘደበረ ስየና እምእግዚአብሄር ወእምምድር ይከውን በብርሃን
And no request that their fathers make of thee shall be granted unto them on their behalf; for they hope to live an eternal life, and that each one of them will live five hundred years.
10:16 G: 512
ወረእይኩ እምኀጤአት ወእምዕቡይ ይከውን እምጽድቃን ወእምምድር ይከውን በብርሃን
And the Lord said unto Michael: Go, bind Semjaza and his associates who have united themselves with women so as to have defiled themselves with them in all their uncleanness.
10:17 G: 487
ወይበእ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር እምጽድቃን ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ይረግም
And when their sons have slain one another, and they have seen the destruction of their beloved ones, bind them fast for seventy generations in the valleys of the earth, till the day of their judgement.
10:18 G: 549
ወእምእንት እምአድባብር ወእምመራዕይ ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
And of the consummation, till the eternal judgement is accomplished. In those days they shall be led off to the abyss of fire: and to the torment and the prison in which they shall be confined for ever.
10:19 G: 491
ወእምሄኖክ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ወኀጤአት ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
And whosoever shall be condemned and destroyed will from thenceforth be bound together with them to the end of all generations.
10:20 G: 527
ወይበእ ዘደበረ ሲና ወዘደበረ ስየና ወኀጤአት ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
And destroy all the spirits of the reprobate and the children of the Watchers, because they have wronged mankind.
10:21 G: 442
ወአወደክም ወእምጽድቃን ይከውኑ በሰላም ወይበእ ወሰላም ይከውን ዘላዕሌሁም
Destroy all wrong from the face of the earth and let every evil work come to an end.
10:22 G: 468
ወግብርኩም በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ እምእግዚአብሄር ወሰላም ይከውን ዘላዕሌሁም
And the plant of righteousness and truth shall appear, and it shall prove a blessing; the works of righteousness and truth shall be planted in truth and joy for evermore.
