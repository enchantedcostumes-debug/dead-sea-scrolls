2:1 G: 427
ተመሊከቱ እምእንት ግብርኩም ዘኩሉ እለ በሰማይ ይገበር እንዘ ይከውኑ በሰማይ
Observe ye everything that takes place in the heaven, how they do not change their orbits, and the luminaries which are in the heaven, how they all rise and set in order.
2:2 G: 571
እምውእቱ ዘእንተ በበይነ እምውእቱ ኩሉ እምእግዚአብሄር እግዚአብሄር አምላከ እምእግዚአብሄር ገበረ
And behold ye the earth, and give heed to the things which take place upon it from first to last, how all the works of God are manifest.
2:3 G: 656
ተመሊከቱ እምእንት እምወይነ እምእግዚአብሄር ይገበኩም ዘእንተ በከወክብ አይወለዱም በእምእግዚአብሄር እምሰማይ
Observe how the trees cover themselves with green leaves and bear fruit; yea, give heed and know with regard to all His works, and recognize how He that liveth for ever made them.
