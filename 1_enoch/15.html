15:1 G: 525
ወይበእ በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ ወኀጤአት ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
And one of the holy ones came to me and waked me, and He made me rise up and approach the door: and I bowed my face downwards.
15:2 G: 482
ተመሊከቱ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር እምጽድቃን በእምእግዚአብሄር ዘእንተ ግብር
And He answered and said to me, and I heard His voice: Fear not, Enoch, thou righteous man and scribe of righteousness: approach hither and hear my voice.
15:3 G: 487
ወእምሄኖክ ወእምጽድቃን ይከውኑ በሰላም ወእምእንት ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ይረግም
And go, say to the Watchers of heaven, who have sent thee to intercede for them: You should intercede for men, and not men for you.
15:4 G: 493
ወረእይኩ እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም ወእምኰን ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
Wherefore have ye left the high, holy, and eternal heaven, and lain with women, and defiled yourselves with the daughters of men and taken to yourselves wives, and done like the children of earth, and begotten giants as your sons?
15:5 G: 423
ተመሊከቱ እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም ወይበእ ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ይረግም
And though ye were holy, spiritual, living the eternal life, you have defiled yourselves with the blood of women, and have begotten children with the blood of flesh, and, as the children of men, have lusted after flesh and blood.
15:6 G: 494
ወበእንተ በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ ወረእይኩ ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
As those also who are mortal, and perishable, therefore have I given them wives also that they might impregnate them, and beget children by them, that thus nothing might be wanting to them on earth.
15:7 G: 529
ወእምእንት እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ወግብርኩም ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
But you were formerly spiritual, living the eternal life, and immortal for all generations of the world.
15:8 G: 470
ወበእንተ ዘደበረ ሲና ወዘደበረ ስየና ተመሊከቱ በእምእግዚአብሄር ዘእንተ ግብር
And therefore I have not appointed wives for you; for as for the spiritual ones of the heaven, in heaven is their dwelling.
15:9 G: 512
ወእምኩሉ በእንተ እምወይነ ይዕሴት ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ወይፈስስ እምዐቡይ ግብር
And now, the giants, who are produced from the spirits and flesh, shall be called evil spirits upon the earth, and on the earth shall be their dwelling.
15:10 G: 493
ተመሊከቱ በእንተ እምወይነ ይዕሴት ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ወእምምድር ይከውን በብርሃን
Evil spirits have proceeded from their bodies; because they are born from men, and from the holy Watchers is their beginning and primal origin.
15:11 G: 516
ወእምኰን እምእግዚአብሄር ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ወሰላም ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
They shall be evil spirits on earth, and evil spirits shall they be called. As for the spirits of heaven, in heaven shall be their dwelling, but as for the spirits of the earth which were born upon the earth, on the earth shall be their dwelling.
15:12 G: 466
ወእምኰን እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም ወኀጤአት በእምእግዚአብሄር ዘእንተ ግብር
And the spirits of the giants afflict, oppress, destroy, attack, do battle, and work destruction on the earth, and cause trouble: they take no food, but nevertheless hunger and thirst, and cause offences.
