28:1 G: 499
ወእምሄኖክ ዘደበረ ሲና ወዘደበረ ስየና ወኀጤአት ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም

28:2 G: 466

28:3 G: 562

Rushing like a copious watercourse which flowed towards the north-west it caused clouds and dew to ascend on every side.
