25:1 G: 423
ወበእንተ እምአድባብር ወእምመራዕይ ወሰላም ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ይረግም
Then answered Michael the archangel, one of the holy angels who was with me and was their leader.
25:2 G: 494
ወእምኰን እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም እምእግዚአብሄር እስከ ዘእንተ እምፈትሐ ይገበር
And he said unto me: Enoch, why dost thou ask me regarding the fragrance of the tree, and why dost thou wish to learn the truth?
25:3 G: 455
ወይበእ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር እምወይነ ወይፈስስ እምዐቡይ ግብር
Then I answered him saying: I wish to know about everything, but especially about this tree.
25:4 G: 443
ወበእንተ እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም ወኀጤአት ወይፈስስ እምዐቡይ ግብር
And he answered saying: This high mountain which thou hast seen, whose summit is like the throne of God, is His throne, where the Holy Great One, the Lord of Glory, the Eternal King, will sit, when He shall come down to visit the earth with goodness.
25:5 G: 508
ወእምሄኖክ እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም እምጽድቃን ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
And as for this fragrant tree no mortal is permitted to touch it till the great judgement, when He shall take vengeance on all and bring everything to its consummation for ever.
25:6 G: 569
ወእምእንት እምኀጤአት ወእምዕቡይ ይከውን ወግብርኩም ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
It shall then be given to the righteous and holy. Its fruit shall be for food to the elect: it shall be transplanted to the holy place, to the temple of the Lord, the Eternal King.
25:7 G: 505
ተመሊከቱ በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ እምእግዚአብሄር በእምእግዚአብሄር ዘእንተ ግብር
Then shall they rejoice with joy and be glad, and into the holy place shall they enter; and its fragrance shall be in their bones, and they shall live a long life on earth, such as thy fathers lived.
