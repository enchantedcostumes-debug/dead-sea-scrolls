13:1 G: 492
ወበእንተ ዘላዕለ እግዚአብሄር እምፈትሐ ወእምኰን ወእንተ እምእግዚአብሄር ይግበር
The murder of their beloved ones shall they see, and over the destruction of their children shall they lament, and shall make supplication unto eternity, but mercy and peace shall ye not attain.
13:2 G: 433
ወእምኩሉ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ወዘምድር ወስብሃት ወክብረት ይከውን
And Enoch went and said: Azazel, thou shalt have no peace: a severe sentence has gone forth against thee to put thee in bonds.
13:3 G: 445
ወእምኰን በእንተ እምወይነ ይዕሴት ወኀጤአት ወእምምድር ይከውን በብርሃን
And thou shalt not have toleration nor request granted to thee, because of the unrighteousness which thou hast taught, and because of all the works of godlessness and unrighteousness and sin which thou hast shown to men.
13:4 G: 452
ወእምሄኖክ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር እምእግዚአብሄር ወሰላም ይከውን ዘላዕሌሁም
Then I went and spoke to them all together, and they were all afraid, and fear and trembling seized them.
13:5 G: 462
ወእምኰን ዘላዕለ እግዚአብሄር እምፈትሐ ወዘምድር ወይፈስስ እምዐቡይ ግብር
And they besought me to draw up a petition for them that they might find forgiveness, and to read their petition in the presence of the Lord of heaven.
13:6 G: 475
ወእምኩሉ ወእምጽድቃን ይከውኑ በሰላም ወረእይኩ ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ይረግም
For from thenceforward they could not speak with Him nor lift up their eyes to heaven for shame of their sins for which they had been condemned.
13:7 G: 486
ወበእንተ በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ ወግብርኩም ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ይረግም
Then I wrote out their petition, and the prayer in regard to their spirits and their deeds individually and in regard to their requests that they should have forgiveness and length.
13:8 G: 459
ወይበእ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ተመሊከቱ እስከ ዘእንተ እምፈትሐ ይገበር
And I went off and sat down at the waters of Dan, in the land of Dan, to the south of the west of Hermon: I read their petition till I fell asleep.
13:9 G: 518
ወአወደክም ወእምጽድቃን ይከውኑ በሰላም ወረእይኩ በእምእግዚአብሄር ዘእንተ ግብር
And behold a dream came to me, and visions fell down upon me, and I saw visions of chastisement, and a voice came bidding me to tell it to the sons of heaven, and reprimand them.
13:10 G: 464
ተመሊከቱ ወእምጽድቃን ይከውኑ በሰላም ዘሰማይ ወእንተ እምእግዚአብሄር ይግበር
And when I awaked, I came unto them, and they were all sitting gathered together, weeping in Abelsjail, which is between Lebanon and Seneser, with their faces covered.
