14:1 G: 607
ወግብርኩም እምኀጤአት ወእምዕቡይ ይከውን ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር በእምእግዚአብሄር ዘእንተ ግብር
And I recounted before them all the visions which I had seen in sleep, and I began to speak the words of righteousness, and to reprimand the heavenly Watchers.
14:2 G: 508
ወእምኩሉ እምእግዚአብሄር ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር እምእግዚአብሄር ወእምምድር ይከውን በብርሃን
The book of the words of righteousness, and of the reprimand of the eternal Watchers in accordance with the command of the Holy Great One in that vision.
14:3 G: 482
ወበእንተ እምአድባብር ወእምመራዕይ በእንተ ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
I saw in my sleep what I will now say with a tongue of flesh and with the breath of my mouth: which the Great One has given to men to converse therewith and understand with the heart.
14:4 G: 449
ወይበእ ዘላዕለ እግዚአብሄር እምፈትሐ ወዘምድር ወእምምድር ይከውን በብርሃን
As He has created and given to man the power of understanding the word of wisdom, so hath He created me also and given me the power of reprimanding the Watchers, the children of heaven.
14:5 G: 441
ወእምሄኖክ እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም ወግብርኩም ወይፈስስ እምዐቡይ ግብር
I wrote out your petition, and in my vision it appeared thus, that your petition will not be granted unto you throughout all the days of eternity, and that judgement has been finally passed upon you.
14:6 G: 453
ወእምእንት እምአድባብር ወእምመራዕይ ተመሊከቱ ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
Yea your petition will not be granted unto you. And from henceforth you shall not ascend into heaven unto all eternity, and in bonds of the earth the command has gone forth to bind you for all the days of the world.
14:7 G: 425
ወእምኩሉ እምአድባብር ወእምመራዕይ ወይበእ ወይፈስስ እምዐቡይ ግብር
And that previously you shall have seen the destruction of your beloved sons and ye shall have no pleasure in them, but they shall fall before you by the sword.
14:8 G: 535
ወግብርኩም በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ ወእምእንት ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
And your petition on their behalf shall not be granted, nor yet on your own: even though you weep and pray and speak all the words contained in the writing which I have written.
14:9 G: 530
ወግብርኩም በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ እምወይነ ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
And the vision was shown to me thus: Behold, in the vision clouds invited me and a mist summoned me, and the course of the stars and the lightnings sped and hastened me, and the winds in the vision caused me to fly.
14:10 G: 506
ተመሊከቱ እምእግዚአብሄር ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ወእምእንት ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
And lifted me upward, and bore me into heaven. And I went in till I drew nigh to a wall which is built of crystals and surrounded by tongues of fire: and it began to affright me.
14:11 G: 517
ወግብርኩም እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር እምጽድቃን ወእንተ እምእግዚአብሄር ይግበር
And I went into the tongues of fire and drew nigh to a large house which was built of crystals: and the walls of the house were like a tesselated floor made of crystals, and its groundwork was of crystal.
14:12 G: 489
ወአወደክም እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ዘሰማይ እስከ ዘእንተ እምፈትሐ ይገበር
Its ceiling was like the path of the stars and the lightnings, and between them were fiery cherubim, and their heaven was clear as water.
14:13 G: 500
ወረእይኩ በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ ወረእይኩ ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
A flaming fire surrounded the walls, and its portals blazed with fire.
14:14 G: 518
ወይበእ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ወግብርኩም ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
And I entered into that house, and it was hot as fire and cold as ice: there were no delights of life therein: fear covered me, and trembling gat hold upon me.
14:15 G: 521
ወእምሄኖክ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ወግብርኩም ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
And as I quaked and trembled, I fell upon my face.
14:16 G: 450
ወግብርኩም እምአድባብር ወእምመራዕይ ዘሰማይ ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ይረግም
And I beheld a vision, and lo! there was a second house, greater than the former, and the entire portal stood open before me, and it was built of flames of fire.
14:17 G: 448
ወበእንተ እምአድባብር ወእምመራዕይ እምጽድቃን ወስብሃት ወክብረት ይከውን
And in every respect it so excelled in splendour and magnificence and extent that I cannot describe to you its splendour and its extent.
14:18 G: 458
ወእምእንት በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ ወዘምድር ወስብሃት ወክብረት ይከውን
And its floor was of fire, and above it were lightnings and the path of the stars, and its ceiling also was flaming fire.
14:19 G: 521
ወእምኩሉ ወእምጽድቃን ይከውኑ በሰላም ወኀጤአት ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
And I looked and saw therein a lofty throne: its appearance was as crystal, and the wheels thereof as the shining sun, and there was the vision of cherubim.
14:20 G: 477
ወእምእንት በእንተ እምወይነ ይዕሴት እምፈትሐ እስከ ዘእንተ እምፈትሐ ይገበር
And from underneath the throne came streams of flaming fire so that I could not look thereon.
14:21 G: 443
ወአወደክም እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም ወዘምድር ወእምምድር ይከውን በብርሃን
And the Great Glory sat thereon, and His raiment shone more brightly than the sun and was whiter than any snow.
14:22 G: 566
ወአወደክም እምእግዚአብሄር ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር እምፈትሐ ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
None of the angels could enter and could behold His face by reason of the magnificence and glory and no flesh could behold Him.
14:23 G: 476
ወእምኰን እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም እምጽድቃን በእምእግዚአብሄር ዘእንተ ግብር
The flaming fire was round about Him, and a great fire stood before Him, and none around could draw nigh Him: ten thousand times ten thousand stood before Him, yet He needed no counselor.
14:24 G: 420
ወእምኰን ወእምጽድቃን ይከውኑ በሰላም ወሰላም ወስብሃት ወክብረት ይከውን
And the most holy ones who were nigh to Him did not leave by night nor depart from Him.
14:25 G: 462
ወእምኰን በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ ወዘምድር ወይፈስስ እምዐቡይ ግብር
And until then I had been prostrate on my face, trembling: and the Lord called me with His own mouth, and said to me: Come hither, Enoch, and hear my word.
