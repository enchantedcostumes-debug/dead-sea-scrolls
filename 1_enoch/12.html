12:1 G: 487
ወበእንተ በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ ወኀጤአት ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ይረግም
And before these things Enoch was hidden, and no one of the children of men knew where he was hidden, and where he abode, and what had become of him.
12:2 G: 446
ወበእንተ በእንተ እምወይነ ይዕሴት ወእምኰን ወይፈስስ እምዐቡይ ግብር
And his activities had to do with the Watchers, and his days were with the holy ones.
12:3 G: 504
ወረእይኩ ዘላዕለ እግዚአብሄር እምፈትሐ ወይበእ እግዚአብሄር ወሰላም ይከውን ዘላዕሌሁም
And I Enoch was blessing the Lord of majesty and the King of the ages, and lo! the Watchers called me -- Enoch the scribe -- and said to me:
12:4 G: 557
ወእምሄኖክ ዘደበረ ሲና ወዘደበረ ስየና እምእግዚአብሄር ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
Enoch, thou scribe of righteousness, go, declare to the Watchers of the heaven who have left the high heaven, the holy eternal place, and have defiled themselves with women.
12:5 G: 502
ወእምኰን እምእግዚአብሄር ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር በእንተ ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
And have done as the children of earth do, and have taken unto themselves wives: Ye have wrought great destruction on the earth.
12:6 G: 454
ወረእይኩ ዘደበረ ሲና ወዘደበረ ስየና ወይበእ ወስብሃት ወክብረት ይከውን
And ye shall have no peace nor forgiveness of sin: and inasmuch as they delight themselves in their children.
