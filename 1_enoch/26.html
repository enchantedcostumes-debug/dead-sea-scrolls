26:1 G: 386
ወእምኩሉ እምክብረት ወእምግብርኩም ወረእይኩ ወሰላም ይከውን ዘላዕሌሁም
And in those days there shall no sorrow or plague or torment or calamity touch them.
26:2 G: 519
ወበእንተ እምመላእክት ዘሰማይ ወዘምድር ወረእይኩ ወይከውኑ እምጽድቃን በእምወይነ
Then I blessed the God of Glory, the Eternal King, who hath prepared such things for the righteous, and hath created them and promised to give to them.
26:3 G: 485
ወእምኩሉ በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ ወግብርኩም ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
And I went from thence to the middle of the earth, and I saw a blessed place in which there were trees with branches abiding and blooming.
26:4 G: 490
ወእምሄኖክ ወእምጽድቃን ይከውኑ በሰላም እምእግዚአብሄር ወስብሃት ወክብረት ይከውን
And there I saw a holy mountain, and underneath the mountain to the east there was a stream and it flowed towards the south.
26:5 G: 441
ወረእይኩ እምአድባብር ወእምመራዕይ ወእምእንት ወስብሃት ወክብረት ይከውን
And I saw towards the east another mountain higher than this, and between them a deep and narrow ravine: in it also ran a stream underneath the mountain.
26:6 G: 508
ወበእንተ በፈትሐ ወበምሐረት ወበጽድቅ እምጽድቃን ወይተወከዱ በምዕራፍ ዘላዕሌሁም
And to the west thereof there was another mountain, lower than the former and of small elevation, and a ravine deep and dry between them: and another deep and dry ravine was at the extremities of the three mountains.
