1:1 G: 462
ቃለ በረከት ዘሄኖክ ሰብእ ጽድቅ ዘበረከ ለጂብዓን እለ ይከውኑ በወይነ ይዕሴት
The words of the blessing of Enoch, wherewith he blessed the elect and righteous, who will be living in the day of tribulation.
1:2 G: 485
ወሰዲቡ ሄኖክ ጽድቅ ሰብእ ወይበእ እመስለ ወእግዚአብሄር እመረለ ለትውልድ ዘይመጹእ
And Enoch a righteous man, whose eyes were opened by God, saw the vision of the Holy One in the heavens, which the angels showed me.
1:3 G: 368
ወእምሄኖክ ሰሚዕኩ እንዘ ይበእ በእንቲሁ በእለ የወይነ ይዕሴት
And from them I heard everything, and from them I understood as I saw, but not for this generation, but for a remote one which is to come.
1:4 G: 467
በእንተ ህሩያን ወጽድቃን ተነገርኩ ወይስመእ ለበወ እግዚአብሄር እምእንት ሰማይ
Concerning the elect I said, and took up my parable concerning them: The Holy Great One will come forth from His dwelling.
1:5 G: 588
ወእምእንት ይከወን እግዚአብሄር እምኰን ዘሰማይ ወይከወን ዘደበረ ሲና ወይረጓ ዘደበረ ስየና
And the God of the world will tread upon the earth on Mount Sinai, and appear from His camp, and appear in the strength of His might from the heaven of heavens.
1:6 G: 353
ወይፈርሁ ኩሉ መላእክት ወይረዐዑ እለ ዘልእለ ከከባት ዘበዐለው
And all shall be smitten with fear and the Watchers shall quake, and great fear and trembling shall seize them unto the ends of the earth.
1:7 G: 544
ወይተንገደገዱ አድባብር ዘዐቡያት ወይመከኩ አኩነ አድባር እስከ ወዲባት እምደር
And the high mountains shall be shaken, and the high hills shall be made low, and shall melt like wax before the flame.
1:8 G: 439
ወትትፈለጥ ምድር ወይፈሰስ ኩሉ ዘደበር ምድር ወይመጹእ ፈትሐ ዘበኩሉ
And the earth shall be wholly rent in sunder, and all that is upon the earth shall perish, and there shall be a judgement upon all.
1:9 G: 538
ወምስለ ጽድቃን ይግበር ሰላም ወይመሐር ዘኩሉ ወይውለዱ ለጽድቃን ሰላም ወይክሉን ኀጤአት ወምሐረት
But with the righteous He will make peace and will protect the elect, and mercy shall be upon them. And they shall all belong to God, and they shall be prospered, and they shall all be blessed.
